Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Click on Sign Up and fill in the required details to create an account. You'll need to verify your email address to complete the registration process.

What if I forget my password?

Use the Forgot Password option on the login page to reset your password. We'll send a secure link to your registered email address.

Is my data secure?

Yes! We follow industry best practices to ensure your data remains safe. All sensitive information is encrypted and we regularly update our security protocols.

How does two-factor authentication work?

Two-factor authentication adds an extra layer of security by requiring something you know (password) and something you have (usually your mobile device). After entering your password, you'll receive a time-sensitive code to complete login.

Can I use AKWORDS on multiple devices?

Yes, you can access your AKWORDS account securely from multiple devices. Our system keeps track of all login attempts and will notify you of any suspicious activity.
